LightSteppe is a [[pyramid|pyramidic]] [[lamp|nightlight lamp]] built from stacked [[translucent]] sheets and [[wood]] [[dowels]]. Its solid pillars seem to loosen under the glow, as if wind is passing through the surface.

The piece invites a kind of tuning: look for energetic vibration in the light, then watch the edges, shadows, and nested planes respond with their own distinct frequency.
